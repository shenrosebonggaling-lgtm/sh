Akala ko dati pare-pareho lang ang mga tao, but I realized hindi pala, nasa ugali talaga. And ikaw, you’re different… in the best way possible. I promise to keep becoming the best version of myself, not just for you but for me too. And I’ll treat you better every day. I know I get a little loud or emotional when I’m jealous—sorry, baby. You make me feel safe in a way I didn’t think I’d feel again. I love you in all ways—on your good days, your bad days, in your silence, and in your chaos. And I’ll keep choosing you, every single day. 💞
